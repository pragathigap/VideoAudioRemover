Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Our Privacy Promise: Your data is yours. All media processing happens locally in your browser. We never upload your files to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Because our tools are browser-based:

  • Media Files: We never upload your video or audio files. Processing is done locally on your device via FFmpeg.wasm.
  • Account Data: Basic usage does not require an account. If you subscribe to a Premium plan, we collect minimal information (email) for authentication and billing.
  • Usage Analytics: We use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to improve our tools. We do not track individual identity or specific file contents.

2. Data Security

Since your media files never reach our servers, the primary security boundary is your own browser's security sandbox. We use industry-standard encryption for any authenticated communication (e.g., login or payments).

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following secure third-party providers:

  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and database management.
  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing. We do not store your credit card details.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@goldenadvertising.in.
